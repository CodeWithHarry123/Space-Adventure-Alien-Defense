How to Play

Destroy Enemies

Shoot down alien ships to earn points. Each enemy destroyed gives you 10 points.

Avoid Collisions

If an enemy hits your spaceship, you lose health. Game over when health reaches zero.

Collect Power-ups

Grab floating power-ups to get health, rapid fire, or temporary shields.

Level Up

Every 100 points advances you to the next level with faster and more enemies.
